I want to go home. What kind of excuses can I make to go home? Use the tool around you to make an excuse. But, wait! Your boss must not know that you are making up your excuses. Try to come up with an excuse whenever the boss is not looking at you. Be creative! Maybe you will come up with a great excuse for your day off.
